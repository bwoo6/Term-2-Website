Overview

On the court, NBA 2K20 is a dazzling experience that matches the energy and presentation of its real-life counterpart. It's mostly relealistic, the tattoos, hairstyles, lightings, and more. Enhanced footwork, dribbling, and player spacing help improve an already sturdy foundation. You can also create custom players, play custom games and control the character. You can play any team in the nba like Golden State Warriors or Los Angeles Clippers, It's your job to control the characters movement, shooting, and dribbling.
